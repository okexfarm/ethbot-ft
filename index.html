Products

HYBRID DEX

The Hybrid Decentralized ProtocolECO-System would create as its Ultimate Product, a Hybrid Decentralized Exchange, combining ERC-721 Minting and trading of Non-Fungible Tokens as well as an integrated DEX Platform for Ethereum Blockchain. It hopes to challenge the near monopoly of OpenSea in the ERC-721 Game and Uniswap in the DEX Game. It shall accomplish this through lower fees, project vetting and margin trading.

NON FUNGIBLE TOKENS

The Hybrid Decentralized Exchange Eco-System would have as part of its eco Product Minting of Non-Fungible Tokens (NFT) to be first sold on OpenSea before the launch and integration of ERC-721 into the HDP Eco-System. HDP would be used for the purpose of minting of Super Rare NFTs.

MULTI COIN WALLET

The Hybrid Decentralized Protocol (HDP) ECO System would include its own Multicoin Wallet, which would go beyond ERC-Tokens and include other blockchains. Its algorithm shall include a portal for instant swaps on the soon to be introduced Hybrid Decentralized Exchange. The swapping fees would be reduced to the barest minimum so as to facilitate mass adoption in a short time frame.

GAMING INTERFACE

A Gaming Interface for the gaming enthusiasts would also be incorporated to add to the utility and appeal of the HDP Eco-System. Such games shall include games such as Ludo, Chess and Horse-Racing

DIGITAL PAYMENT GATEWAY

It also seeks to incorporate a Digital Payment Gateway to facilitate instant Crypto/Fait Swap. To achieve this, partnerships and legal consultation especially when navigating the terrains of the Securities and exchange commission of the United States of America (USA) as well as the European Union (EU) as it involves the production and coding of Cards.

MAINNET

A Mainnet for the Hybrid Decentralized Exchange Eco-System would later come in play. A Collaboration with Universa, an extremely fast and secure enterprise-level blockchain would be sought. This is because it is the fastest blockchain protocol, processing an average of 20,000 transactions per second. This figure can and has been scaled upwards.
